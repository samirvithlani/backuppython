operator: operators are the symbols that use to perform the operations on the operands.

unary operator: unary operators are the operators that perform the operations on the single operand.
binary operator: binary operators are the operators that perform the operations on the two operands. 
ternary operator: ternary operators are the operators that perform the operations on the three operands.

binary:
arithmetic operators: +, -, *, /, %,,**, // 
logical operators: and or not
relational operators: <, >, <=, >=, ==, !=
assignment operators: =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=,
bitwise operators: &, |, ^, ~, <<, >>

membership operators: in, not in
identity operators: is, is not
